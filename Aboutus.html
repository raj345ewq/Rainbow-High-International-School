About Us

Rainbow High International School is one of the oldest schools of our country and it is one of the first two International schools established in the state of Karnataka in the year 1965. It is a pioneer institution run by Christ University, an autonomous body, under the Ministry of Private Resources, Govt. of India. It has been functioning in its own building having a very big attractive campus, catering to the educational needs of the children of the transferable Central Govt. employees including defence personnel, State Govt. employees and the floating population.
The school is located in the western end of the town of Bangalore, which is located on the Chennai - Mumbai highway and is connected by railway line from Bangalore to Puducherry and Mysuru.
